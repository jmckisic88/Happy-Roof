Process

What does the roof replacement process look like?

Our process includes: free inspection, detailed written estimate, material selection, permit pulling, full tear-off of the old roof, installation of new underlayment and roofing material, final building department inspection, and thorough cleanup. Most residential roofs are completed in 1–3 days.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential shingle roof replacements take 1–3 days depending on size and complexity. Metal roofs typically take 3–5 days. Tile roofs can take 5–7 days. Weather delays are possible during Florida's rainy season, but we plan accordingly and keep you informed.

Do I need to be home during the roof replacement?

No. As long as we have access to the property and any gates, you do not need to be home. We'll keep you updated throughout the process via phone or text and send photos when the job is complete.

Does Happy Roof pull permits?

Yes. Happy Roof pulls all required building permits for every roof replacement and handles scheduling the final inspection with the local building department. This is included in every project at no extra charge. A permitted roof protects your home's resale value and insurance coverage.

Will you remove and dispose of my old roof?

Yes. Every roof replacement includes full tear-off, disposal of old materials, and thorough cleanup of your property. We use magnetic nail sweepers to ensure no nails or debris are left behind in your yard or driveway.

Materials

What roofing materials does Happy Roof install?

We install GAF Timberline HDZ and UHDZ shingles, Owens Corning Duration and Oakridge shingles, standing seam and corrugated metal roofing, clay and concrete tile, and flat roof systems including TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen. All materials come with manufacturer warranties.

What is the best roofing material for Florida?

Architectural shingles (like GAF Timberline HDZ) are the most popular and cost-effective choice for Florida homes. Metal roofing offers superior wind resistance and longevity. Tile is ideal for Mediterranean and Spanish-style homes. The best choice depends on your budget, aesthetic preference, and HOA requirements. We'll help you choose during your free estimate.

What is the difference between 3-tab and architectural shingles?

Architectural (dimensional) shingles are thicker, more durable, and offer better wind resistance (up to 130 mph) compared to basic 3-tab shingles. They also have a more dimensional, textured appearance and come with longer manufacturer warranties. Happy Roof recommends architectural shingles for all Florida homes due to our severe weather conditions.

Do you install metal roofs?

Yes. We install standing seam and corrugated metal roofing in a wide variety of colors and profiles. Metal roofs offer excellent wind resistance (up to 160+ mph), energy efficiency, and can last 40–70 years with proper maintenance. They're a great long-term investment for Florida homeowners.

Can you match my existing tile if I need a repair?

In most cases, yes. We work with multiple tile suppliers and can source matching or closely matching tiles for repairs. For discontinued profiles, we'll find the closest available match and discuss options with you before proceeding.

Warranties

What warranty does Happy Roof offer on workmanship?

Happy Roof provides a 15-year workmanship warranty on all shingle, metal, and tile roof installations, and a 10-year workmanship warranty on flat roof systems. This is provided in writing on every single project — no exceptions. Most contractors in the Tampa Bay area offer only 1–5 years.

What do manufacturer warranties cover?

Manufacturer warranties cover defects in the roofing material itself — such as premature cracking, curling, or granule loss not caused by weather events. As a GAF Certified Installer and Owens Corning Preferred Contractor, we can offer enhanced manufacturer warranties including GAF's Lifetime Shingle Warranty and Owens Corning's Lifetime Limited Warranty.

What is NOT covered by the workmanship warranty?

Our workmanship warranty does not cover damage caused by hurricanes, storms, falling trees, hail, improper modifications made by others after installation, lack of maintenance, or normal wear and tear. It specifically covers defects in our installation workmanship — if something we installed fails under normal conditions, we fix it at no cost.

How do I file a warranty claim?

Call us at (813) 595-7663 or email info@happyroof.com. We'll schedule an inspection to assess the issue. If it's covered under our workmanship warranty, we'll repair it at no cost to you. We aim to respond to all warranty claims within 48 hours.

Insurance & Storm Damage

Does Happy Roof help with insurance claims?

Yes. We assist Tampa Bay homeowners with the full insurance claim process for storm damage — from the initial roof inspection and damage documentation to working directly with your insurance adjuster. Our initial storm damage inspection is always free.

How do I know if my roof has storm damage?

Signs of storm damage include missing or lifted shingles, dents in metal flashing or vents, cracked tiles, excessive granule loss in gutters, water stains on interior ceilings, and dented gutters or downspouts. If you suspect damage after a storm, call (813) 595-7663 for a free inspection.

Should I file an insurance claim or pay out of pocket?

It depends on the extent of damage and your deductible. If storm damage is widespread or affects a significant portion of your roof, filing a claim is usually the right move. We'll inspect your roof for free and give you an honest assessment before you make any decisions. We never pressure homeowners to file unnecessary claims.

Does Happy Roof do emergency tarping after a storm?

Yes. We provide emergency tarping to prevent further water intrusion and interior damage while your insurance claim is processed or your replacement is scheduled. Call (813) 595-7663 for urgent storm damage assistance.
