Leak Detection

Roof leaks are rarely where they appear inside your home. Water travels along rafters and decking before dripping through your ceiling — sometimes feet or yards from the actual entry point. Our experienced technicians trace leaks to their source using systematic inspection methods, checking flashing, pipe boots, valleys, ridge vents, and penetration points. We find the real problem and fix it right the first time.

Shingle Replacement

Missing, cracked, curling, or wind-damaged shingles leave your roof deck exposed to water and UV damage. We replace damaged shingles with matching products — same brand, same color line, same profile — so the repair blends seamlessly with your existing roof. For older roofs where an exact match isn't available, we'll discuss color-match options to get as close as possible.

Flashing Repair

Flashing failures are one of the most common causes of roof leaks. The metal flashing around chimneys, skylights, walls, pipes, and valleys deteriorates over time from expansion and contraction, UV exposure, and corrosion. We repair and replace flashing with proper materials and techniques — including step flashing, counter flashing, and drip edge — to create a watertight seal that lasts.

Storm Damage Repair

Tampa Bay's hurricane season brings high winds, heavy rain, and flying debris that can cause significant roof damage. Happy Roof provides comprehensive storm damage repair including emergency tarping, debris removal, structural assessment, and permanent restoration. We also offer full insurance claim assistance — from the initial damage documentation to working directly with your adjuster — at no additional cost to you.

Pipe Boot & Vent Repair

The rubber boots around plumbing vents are one of the first components to fail on a Florida roof. UV exposure causes them to crack and split within 8 to 12 years, creating a direct path for water into your attic. We replace failed pipe boots with high-quality replacements and check all roof penetrations during every repair visit.

Honest Advice

WHEN TO REPAIR VS. REPLACE

Not every damaged roof needs to be replaced. At Happy Roof, we believe in giving you an honest assessment — even when a full replacement would mean more revenue for us. Here's how we think about it:

Repair makes sense when: Your roof is under 15 years old, the damage is localized to one area, the underlying decking is sound, and the repair cost is well below 30% of a full replacement. A well-executed repair can add years of life to your existing roof without the expense and disruption of a full tear-off.

Replacement makes sense when: Your roof is 20+ years old, you're seeing widespread granule loss, multiple leak areas, sagging or soft spots in the decking, or the repair estimate exceeds 30% of what a new roof would cost. In these cases, continuing to patch an aging system becomes more expensive than starting fresh.

We'll inspect your roof, show you photos of what we find, and give you our honest recommendation. If a $500 repair will solve your problem, that's what we'll tell you. Period.
