Common Questions

FINANCING FAQ

Will checking my rate hurt my credit score?

No. The initial rate check uses a soft credit inquiry, which does not affect your credit score. Only if you accept an offer and formally apply will a hard pull occur — and only from the lender you choose.

How much can I finance?

Loan amounts vary based on your creditworthiness and the lender. Enhancify works with a network of lenders that can fund roofing projects of various sizes — from minor repairs to full commercial replacements. Check your rate to see what you qualify for.

How soon after approval can my project start?

Once your loan is approved and finalized, Happy Roof can schedule your project immediately. In many cases, we're able to get started within days of your loan funding. We'll coordinate the timing with you.

Can I finance storm or insurance damage repairs?

Yes. Financing can be used for any roofing project — including storm damage repairs — even if insurance is paying a portion. Financing can cover deductibles, non-covered items, or upgrades beyond what insurance allows.

Do I need perfect credit to qualify?

Not at all. Enhancify works with a diverse network of lenders, including options for a range of credit profiles. The best way to find out what you qualify for is to check your rate — it's free and takes 60 seconds.

Is Happy Roof the lender?

No. Happy Roof is a roofing contractor, not a lender. We've partnered with Enhancify, a licensed financing platform, to connect our customers with third-party lenders. All credit decisions are made by the lender, not by us.
