WHY PALM HARBOR HOMEOWNERS CHOOSE HAPPY ROOF

Palm Harbor is one of the most desirable unincorporated communities in Pinellas County — a collection of established neighborhoods, planned communities, and waterfront homes stretching from the Intracoastal Waterway inland to the East Lake corridor. From the golf-course homes of Innisbrook to the family-oriented streets of Lansbrook and the established subdivisions near Curlew Road, Palm Harbor homes represent a significant investment that demands proper roofing protection. Many homes here were built during the construction boom of the 1980s and 1990s, which means thousands of roofs are now at or beyond their expected lifespan.

Happy Roof serves Palm Harbor from our nearby headquarters in Oldsmar — just minutes away. We understand Pinellas County's permitting requirements, know the common roof styles and ages in the area, and are equipped to handle everything from a straightforward shingle replacement on a ranch home to a complex tile-to-metal conversion on a larger estate. We hold Florida Roofing Contractor License #CCC1337380, carry complete insurance coverage, and back every installation with a 15-year written workmanship warranty.
