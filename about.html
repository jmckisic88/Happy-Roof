What We Stand For

OUR VALUES.

Honesty First

We tell you what your roof actually needs — not what makes us the most money. If it only needs a repair, we say repair. No upselling, ever.

We Show Up

When we schedule a visit, we are there. If something changes, you hear from us — not a no-show. Reliability isn't extra, it's the baseline.

Quality Materials

We only install products we'd put on our own homes. Every material we use comes with a manufacturer warranty — because quality starts with what you build with.

Local Team, Local Knowledge

Our crews live and work here. We know Tampa Bay's building codes, permit requirements, HOA rules, and what Florida weather actually does to a roof.

We Clean Up After Ourselves

Every nail, every scrap. We use magnet rollers and fully clear your yard before we leave. You shouldn't have to worry about what we leave behind.

We Stand Behind Our Work

Happy Roof guarantees its Shingle, Metal, and Tile Workmanship for 15 years & its Flat Roof Installation for 10 years — in writing, every time. If something goes wrong because of how it was installed, we make it right. Period.
