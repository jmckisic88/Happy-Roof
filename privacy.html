Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026  ·  Happy Roof LLC, Tampa Bay, FL

This Privacy Policy describes how Happy Roof LLC ("Happy Roof," "we," "us," or "our"), a roofing contractor based in Tampa Bay, Florida, collects, uses, and shares your information when you visit our website, contact us, or request our services. By using our website or submitting a form, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Contact information: name, phone number, email address, and property address
  • Service details: type of roofing service requested, preferred materials, and project notes
  • Communication records: messages you send us via forms, email, or text
  • Referral information: if you were referred by another customer

We may also automatically collect limited technical data such as browser type and general location when you visit our website, solely for analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to estimate requests and schedule appointments
  • Communicate with you about your roofing project
  • Send transactional or informational text messages and calls (if you opt in)
  • Improve our services and website
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. SMS / Text Messaging Program

SMS Consent & Opt-In

By checking the SMS consent box on our estimate or contact forms, you expressly consent to receive text messages from Happy Roof LLC at the mobile number you provide. These messages may include:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Estimate follow-ups and project updates
  • Responses to your service inquiries
  • Promotional offers related to roofing services (occasional)

Message frequency varies based on your inquiry and project status. During an active project you may receive up to 4–6 messages per week.

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

How to Opt Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any text message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive a single confirmation message and no further texts will be sent except as required by law.

To re-subscribe, text START to the same number.

Help

For assistance, reply HELP to any message or contact us directly at info@happyroof.com or (813) 595-7663.

Carriers

Supported carriers include but are not limited to: AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, Sprint, Boost, U.S. Cellular, and MetroPCS. Carrier liability is not assumed by Happy Roof LLC.

No Purchase Required

Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services from Happy Roof LLC.

4. Phone & Email Communications

By providing your phone number or email address, you also consent to be contacted by Happy Roof via phone call or email regarding your service request. You may opt out of email marketing by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email we send, or by contacting us directly. Phone calls for transactional purposes (scheduling, follow-up) are not subject to the same opt-out, but we will honor any do-not-call requests.

5. Information Sharing

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Third-party tools we use to operate our business (e.g., scheduling software, financing partner Enhancify) under confidentiality agreements
  • Legal compliance: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger or acquisition, your information may be transferred as a business asset

We never sell your personal data to data brokers, marketers, or other third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, provide ongoing services, and comply with legal obligations — typically no longer than 5 years after your last interaction with us. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8).

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS, phone, or email marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@happyroof.com or (813) 595-7663. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., Google Reviews, Enhancify financing). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website or services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
