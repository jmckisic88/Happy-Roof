## Growth Tactics for Starting from Zero

1. **First 30 days goal:** 25 posts, 100 followers per platform
2. **Engage locally:** Comment on Tampa community pages, neighborhood groups, local business accounts — not to sell, but to be visible
3. **Use location tags:** Always tag Tampa, specific neighborhoods (Westchase, Carrollwood, South Tampa, Clearwater, etc.)
4. **Hashtag strategy:** Mix of #TampaRoofer #RoofingTikTok #TampaHomeowner #FloridaRoofing #RoofReplacement #StormDamage #HappyRoof + trending audio on TikTok
5. **Cross-post everything:** Every TikTok → IG Reel → FB Reel. Repurpose carousels into FB albums.
6. **Reply to EVERY comment and DM** in the first 90 days — algorithm rewards engagement velocity
7. **Post at peak times:** Tampa audience → 7–8 AM (before work), 12–1 PM (lunch), 6–8 PM (evening scroll)
