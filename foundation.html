Our Mission

NO FAMILY SHOULD LIVE UNDER A
FAILING ROOF.

We started Happy Roof because we believe in doing things the right way. But we also know that the families who need a safe roof the most are often the ones who can't afford it.

Every Shingle Heart is our answer to that. We set aside materials, labor, and time every quarter to provide completely free roof repairs and replacements to qualifying households in the Tampa Bay area — no strings attached.

The Process

HOW EVERY SHINGLE HEART WORKS

Step 1

Apply or Nominate

Households in need can apply directly or be nominated by a neighbor, friend, church, or community organization. Anyone can submit — you don't have to know us.

Step 2

We Review & Inspect

Our team reviews all applications with compassion and confidentiality. Selected families receive a free in-person inspection to assess the scope of work needed.

Step 3

We Get to Work

Approved families receive their roof repair or replacement completely free of charge. Our crew shows up with the same quality materials and craftsmanship as every other job.

Common Questions

ABOUT THE FOUNDATION

Who qualifies for a free roof?

We look at financial need, safety risk, and household circumstances. There's no single qualifier — we review each case with the full picture in mind. Homeowners, long-term renters, seniors, veterans, and families with children are all welcome to apply.

Is there really no cost to the recipient?

Absolutely zero. Materials, labor, cleanup — everything is covered. We don't ask for anything in return. This is a gift, not a loan or a program with hidden terms.

How are applications reviewed?

Our team reviews all submissions personally. We look at need, safety, and the information provided. Approved applicants are contacted to schedule a free in-person inspection before any work is planned.

Can I nominate someone anonymously?

You can indicate whether you'd like your identity kept private. We'll need your contact info to follow up, but we can keep it out of any communications with the nominee. We'll always reach out to you first before contacting the person you nominated.

Where do donations go?

100% of donations go directly toward materials and any associated costs for recipient roofing projects. Happy Roof LLC covers all administrative and labor costs so your donation does the most good possible. For any questions, email info@happyroof.com.

How often do you select recipients?

We plan to review and select recipients on a quarterly basis. As the program grows and funding increases, we hope to expand the frequency and number of families we can help each year.
