Systems We Install

COMMERCIAL ROOFING SYSTEMS

TPO Roofing

Thermoplastic polyolefin (TPO) is the most popular commercial roofing membrane in Florida — and for good reason. TPO is highly reflective, reducing cooling costs by up to 30% in Tampa Bay's intense heat. It resists UV degradation, punctures, and chemical exposure, making it ideal for flat and low-slope commercial buildings. Happy Roof installs mechanically fastened and fully adhered TPO systems from leading manufacturers with 20 to 30-year material warranties. Our heat-welded seams create a watertight bond stronger than the membrane itself, providing superior leak protection for your commercial property.

EPDM Roofing

Ethylene propylene diene monomer (EPDM) is a proven synthetic rubber membrane with a track record spanning decades. EPDM is exceptionally durable, flexible, and resistant to ozone and weathering. It performs well on flat and low-slope roofs and is available in black (for heat absorption in cooler applications) or white (for reflectivity). Happy Roof installs ballasted, mechanically attached, and fully adhered EPDM systems with long-term material warranties. EPDM is a cost-effective choice for warehouses, industrial buildings, and large commercial properties throughout Tampa Bay.

Modified Bitumen

Modified bitumen roofing systems combine the reliability of traditional built-up roofing with the ease of modern installation methods. These multi-layer systems offer excellent waterproofing, impact resistance, and flexibility — critical for Tampa Bay's temperature swings and severe storm exposure. Happy Roof installs torch-applied, cold-applied, and self-adhered modified bitumen systems from top manufacturers. Modified bitumen is an excellent choice for commercial buildings that need a robust, proven flat roof system with built-in redundancy.

Commercial Metal Roofing

Standing seam and R-panel metal roofing systems deliver unmatched durability for commercial buildings. Metal roofs last 40 to 60 years, reflect solar heat to reduce energy costs, and provide superior wind resistance for Florida's hurricane season. Happy Roof installs Gulf Coast Supply panels including GulfLok, GulfSeam, GulfRib, and MegaLoc — all engineered for Florida's climate and building codes. Metal is ideal for retail centers, churches, restaurants, and any commercial building that values long-term performance.

How It Works

1

Site Assessment & Proposal

We conduct a thorough assessment of your commercial roof, including core samples if needed, drainage evaluation, and structural review. You receive a detailed proposal with system options, material specifications, project timeline, and pricing. No pressure, no hidden costs.

2

System Design & Permitting
