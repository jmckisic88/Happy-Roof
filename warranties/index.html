The Details

WHAT'S COVERED & WHAT'S NOT

What's Covered

Leaks caused by installation defects or workmanship errors

Improperly installed flashing, underlayment, or drip edge

Nail pops or fastener failures from our installation

Vent boot, pipe collar, or penetration seal failures

Ridge cap or hip cap installation defects

Valley, step flashing, and counter flashing issues

What's Not Covered

Damage caused by hurricanes, storms, or falling debris

Modifications or repairs made by others after installation

Damage from lack of maintenance or neglect

Normal wear and tear over time

Structural movement or settling of the building

Material defects (covered separately by manufacturer warranty)
