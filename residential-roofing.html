Roofing Materials

MATERIALS WE INSTALL

Asphalt Shingles

The most popular choice for Tampa Bay homes. We install GAF Timberline HDZ and UHDZ, Owens Corning Duration and Oakridge, and other premium architectural shingles rated for 130+ MPH winds. Shingle roofs offer excellent value, a wide range of color options, and proven performance in Florida's climate. Typical lifespan is 25 to 30 years with proper installation and ventilation.

25–30 Year Lifespan

Metal Roofing

Standing seam and exposed-fastener metal roofs are becoming the preferred choice for Florida homeowners who want maximum durability and energy efficiency. Metal reflects solar heat, reducing cooling costs by up to 25%. We install Gulf Coast Supply panels — GulfLok, GulfSeam, GulfSnap, GulfRib, and MegaLoc — all engineered for Florida's wind and rain. Metal roofs last 40 to 60 years and often qualify for insurance discounts.

40–60 Year Lifespan

Tile Roofing

Concrete and clay tile roofs deliver a classic Florida aesthetic with exceptional longevity. We install barrel tile, S-tile, and flat tile profiles from Verea and other top manufacturers. Tile is naturally fire-resistant, energy-efficient, and can withstand sustained high winds when properly installed. Tile roofs can last 50 years or more and add significant curb appeal and resale value to your home.

50+ Year Lifespan

How It Works

OUR 4-STEP PROCESS

1

Free Inspection & Estimate

We start with a thorough roof inspection — checking decking, underlayment, flashing, ventilation, and overall condition. You receive a detailed, written estimate with material options, timelines, and pricing. No pressure, no hidden fees, no obligation.

2

Material Selection & Permits

Once you approve the scope, we help you select the ideal material and color for your home. We then pull all required permits with your local building department and order materials directly from the manufacturer. You pick the start date that works for you.

3

Professional Installation

Our crew performs the full tear-off, inspects and repairs the decking as needed, installs new underlayment and flashing, and lays your new roofing system to manufacturer specifications. We protect your landscaping, driveway, and property throughout the process.

4

Final Inspection & Warranty

After installation, we schedule the county inspection to verify code compliance. Once passed, we deliver your warranty documentation — including our 15-year workmanship warranty and the manufacturer material warranty. We follow up at 30 days and 1 year to make sure you're satisfied.
